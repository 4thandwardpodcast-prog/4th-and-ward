About the Show
Like a sports bar conversation — except we actually know football.

4th & Ward is a sports and culture podcast hosted by brothers Jack and Adam Wardlaw. Every week they break down football, argue about takes, celebrate small-town teams, and go deep on the stories that make sports worth talking about.

Jack brings the coaching perspective. Adam brings the chaos. Together it's part X's and O's breakdown, part locker room humor, part two brothers who grew up around the game.
